FPSO Operations and Maintenance

NIQ has solid knowledge of all systems (the topsides, Hull and Structure, etc. integrated into an FPSO) with an understanding of the different equipment operating conditions, requirements, interaction and dependences to support ensuring the smooth day-to-day running (operation and maintenance) of the FPSO

FPSO Services we offer :

Minimize downtime.

Increase safety levels and ensuring compliance to environmental guidelines.

Optimize production performance at lower costs.

Reduce maintenance cost over the lifetime of the equipment.

Delivering cost-efficient state-of-the-art solutions to FPSO operations.

NIQ has the capacity to support operations readiness and assurance for a new FPSO to be tied to a field by developing or upgrading safety management systems, Integrity management Plan, preparing operational philosophy, guidelines, and procedures.

We support the Planning, Scheduling, Management and Optimisation of all major maintenance activities including preventive maintenance, planned shutdown and unscheduled repairs during the operations and maintenance phase.

Risk based Inspection of an FPSO

This plays a significant role in the prevention of failures. Inspection programs are established to detect and evaluate deterioration due to in-service operation.

Our risk-based Inspection approach combines factors such as satisfactory operating experience, low deterioration rates, minimal consequences of failure, and condition-based inspection interval setting.
