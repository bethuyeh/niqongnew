Corporate Social Responsibility

NIQ Oil and Gas Corporate Social Responsibility is focused on sustainability, we believe that behaving responsibly is critical to the sustainability of our organization which is why we are committed to looking after the health safety, and development of our people. We work towards enhancing and protecting our reputation with stakeholders, to contribute positively to the host communities, and to minimize our environmental footprint.

Our CSR framework is focused on providing support to members of our host communities through the Community Relations and Development Department. We have supported the improvement of infrastructure, encouraging local enterprise, facilitating know-how, and building capacity for the sustainable use of resources in our host communities, these activities have fostered harmonious relationships with our host communities.
